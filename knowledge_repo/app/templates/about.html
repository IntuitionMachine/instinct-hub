Discover Radeon Instinct™ for Deep Learning Solutions

Radeon Instinct™ is the fusion of human instinct and machine intelligence, designed to be open from the metal forward. Higher levels of datacenter performance and efficiencies are enabled through AMD’s introduction of world-class GPU technologies and the Radeon Instinct’s open ecosystem approach to datacenter design through our ROCm software platform, support of various system architectures, and industry standard interconnect technologies.

This application allows to try out live examples of Deep Learning solutions that run on Radeon Instinct™. The following features are available:

  • Curated Deep Learning solutions that have been validated on Radeon Instinct™ GPUs.
  • Users can run, experiment and interact with these solutions using a web browser.
  • Users can re-train these solutions to optimize them for their custom requirements.
  • Users can browse and search the curated solutions.
  • Users can visualize the training and the inference results.
